Who Am I?

Master of Science in Law student at Northwestern Graduation 2021 and a Benchling

March 11, 2021 Aishwarya Raj

By day, I work on a cloud based informatics R & D platform to accelerate life sciences worflows and results. By night, I deep dive into areas such as data privacy, regulatory frameworks and intellectual property. I am passionate and curious about the intersection of data, biotechnology, and regulation to see how we can enable and preserve security and privacy as we drive analysis and innovation.

While you are here, feel free to take a look around, click on a few things I have been working on or take a look at my work, projects, or personal endeavors.

Analyzing Global Cosmetics Regulations

Northwestern Master of Science in Law Symposium 2021

May 2021 Aishwarya Raj

This presentation will compare regulatory frameworks governing cosmetics in different jurisdictions. It will look at the scope of regulations, how those regulations apply, and at the impact of regulations on product safety.

Presentation on Healthcare Regulation

Northwestern Master of Science in Law Symposium 2020

May 2020 Aishwarya Raj

This presentation highlights the changing needs of the pharmaceutical industry as well as various regulatory timelines and stipulations for different pharmaceutical products. The COVID-19 pandemic will provide some context for this exploration.
